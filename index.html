Let me introduce myself

Hi, I'm Romina, a self-motivated software developer with a robust foundation in programming, user experience, and web development. Currently pursuing my second Master’s in Computer Science with a focus on Software Engineering at Johannes Kepler University (JKU), I hold both a Bachelor’s and a Master’s in Computer Engineering from Polytechnic University in Tirana.
My professional journey includes creative problem-solving skills applied in research on deadlocks with Prof. Dr. Igli Tafa and co-authoring a paper on machine learning applications in life cycle assessments. I've developed practical solutions such as an employee app released on Google Play and the App Store, and an accessibility hub at TeamViewer to enhance cross-team collaboration.
As a self-taught UX/UI designer with a year of professional experience, I am proficient in Agile methodologies, DevOps practices, and technical documentation. My passion for human-computer interaction and accessible web design has driven my active participation in accessibility conferences and ongoing research. I am dedicated to making the digital world more inclusive by focusing on user-friendly and accessible web applications.
